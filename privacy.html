Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

We collect your email address solely for waitlist notification. As a "Zero-Friction" tool, your core decluttering data is stored locally via AsyncStorage and never hits our servers.

Dusty uses a hybrid pipeline. Basic detection happens on your phone. Only intentional "Ask Dusty" scans are processed via Gemini 1.5 Flash for valuation. Photos are not permanently stored.

3. Tracking & Analytics

We use minimalist analytics to monitor app stability. We do not engage in the "shady" metadata harvesting common in 3-star cleanup apps.
