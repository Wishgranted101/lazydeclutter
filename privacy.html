Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect your email address solely for the purpose of waitlist notification and beta access. We do not sell your email to third parties.

2. AI Image Processing

LazyDeclutter uses a hybrid vision pipeline. Preliminary object detection occurs locally on your device. When you use the "Ask Dusty" feature, specific item data is sent to the Gemini 1.5 Flash API for valuation. Your photos are not stored on our servers permanently.

3. Local Storage

To maintain a zero-friction experience, all "Dopamine Tally" history and calendar data are stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage. This data disappears if the app is deleted.[1]
